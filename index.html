ABOUT US

Introduction

African Site Masters Limited (ASM) is a fully Zambian-owned construction company dedicated to providing top-tier construction services. We specialize in various aspects of construction, including road works, structural engineering, and building development. Our goal is to enhance the construction industry in Zambia by maintaining high standards, ensuring safety, and fostering innovation.

ASM is committed to continuously improving the quality of its services by investing in skilled personnel and modern technology. Our mission is to be the preferred general contractor in the region by upholding ethical business practices and ensuring client satisfaction.

Core Values

  • Y Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency.
  • Y Quality: We strive for excellence in every project we undertake.
  • Y Professionalism: We uphold high standards in all aspects of our operations.
  • Y Safety: We prioritize the health and well-being of our employees and clients.
  • Y Innovation: We embrace modern technology and creative solutions to enhance productivity.
  • Y Sustainability: We ensure environmentally friendly construction practices.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to deliver high-quality construction services with professionalism and integrity. We aim to build long-lasting relationships with our clients by providing reliable and innovative solutions that meet international standards and contribute to the growth and development of communities.

Vision Statement

To be a leading construction company in Zambia and beyond, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability in the industry. We aspire to continuously improve infrastructure, enhance project efficiency, and deliver world-class construction solutions.

Company Objectives

  • Y To improve construction quality and productivity to levels comparable with other leading construction markets.
  • Y To enhance the capabilities and professionalism of personnel in the industry and promote the export of construction services.
  • Y To ensure construction safety, well-maintained structures, and efficient project execution under a progressive regulatory framework.
  • Y To be a technical authority and government advisor on structural and civil defense shelter engineering.
  • Y To foster teamwork, innovation, and the professional development of our workforce to their maximum potential.

SAFETY

Our Health & Safety Statement:

Establish and implement a Health and Safety Management System to manage the risks associated with our premises and activities.

Regularly monitor our performance and revise our Health and Safety Management System as necessary to ensure we achieve our objective of continuous improvement.

Provide sufficient resources to meet the requirements of current Health and Safety legislation and aim to achieve the standards of 'Good Practice' applicable to our activities.

Actively promote an open attitude to Health and Safety issues, encouraging staff to identify and report hazards so that we can all contribute to creating and maintaining a safe working environment.

Communicate and consult with our staff on all issues affecting their health and safety and, in doing so, bring this policy to their attention. Provide adequate training for our staff to enable them to work safely and effectively, and to ensure they are competent and confident as they carry out their job assignments.

Carry out and regularly review risk assessments to identify hazards and existing control measures. We will priorities, plan and complete any corrective actions required to reduce risk to an acceptable level. Maintain our premises and work equipment to a standard that ensures that risks are effectively managed. Ensure that responsibilities for Health and Safety are allocated, understood, monitored and fulfilled.

Provide health surveillance for staff where appropriate and maintain records. It is the duty of all of us when at work:

  • Y To take reasonable care of our own safety
  • Y To take reasonable care of the safety of others who may be affected by what we do or fail to do
  • Y To co-operate so that we can all comply with our legal duties
  • Y To ensure we do not interfere with or misuse anything provided in the interests of health and safety.
